How does it work?

The DC25 is a professional networking club designed to bring together top performing young professionals in the DC area from various industries to encourage cross-industry networking and collaboration. If you're a talented and busy young professional looking to expand your social and professional circle, look no further -- DC25 will take care of you. In the town that loves to brunch, we organize small brunches to help you meet new, awesome people around town. After getting to know you, we set you up with a group of like minded individuals. We know you're busy, so just sign up, show up and we guarantee you'll have an amazing time, with amazing people.
